Sunway Lagoon

We are the one-stop place for fun over 90 attractions spread across 88 acres, Sunway Lagoon provides the ultimate theme park experience in 5 adventure zones — Water Park, Amusement Park, Wildlife Park, Extreme Park & Scream Park . Come experience them all by booking your tickets online.

Once you step in, you can spend the entire day here! Explore the various dining options available at the theme park. If you need some place to sleep, we are just next door to the Sunway Resort Hotel & Spa.

We also play host to some of the hottest acts and global events so stay tuned for upcoming events and promotion by following Sunway Lagoon on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.
